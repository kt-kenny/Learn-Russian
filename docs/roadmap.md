# 6-Month Roadmap to A2

Assumes ~30–45 min/day. Each phase ends with a self-check; don't advance until you pass it.

## Phase 1 — Foundations (Weeks 1–4)

| Week | Grammar | Phrases | Daily |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Alphabet & pronunciation rules](alphabet.md) | [Survival essentials](phrases/01-survival.md) | Flashcards: Survival deck |
| 2 | [Gender & nouns](grammar/01-gender-and-nouns.md) | [Greetings & small talk](phrases/02-greetings-smalltalk.md) | Flashcards + read aloud 5 min |
| 3 | [Accusative case](grammar/02-accusative.md) | review | [Case drills §1](practice/case-drills.md) |
| 4 | [Prepositional case](grammar/03-prepositional.md) | [Food & restaurants](phrases/03-food-restaurant.md) | Case drills §2 |

**Self-check:** read a paragraph of simple Russian aloud without stopping; say "I see the dog / I'm in the park" with correct endings.

## Phase 2 — The Case System (Weeks 5–10)

| Week | Grammar | Extras |
|---|---|---|
| 5–6 | [Genitive](grammar/04-genitive.md) — the workhorse case: possession, «у меня есть», negation, numbers | Genitive drills |
| 7 | [Dative](grammar/05-dative.md) — giving, age, «мне нравится» | [Directions & transport](phrases/04-directions-transport.md) |
| 8 | [Instrumental](grammar/06-instrumental.md) — "with", professions | Case drills §3 |
| 9–10 | Consolidation: mixed case drills, all six cases | [Shopping & money](phrases/05-shopping-money.md) |

**Self-check:** decline «новая книга» in all six cases; translate 10 mixed-case sentences from the drills cold.

## Phase 3 — Verbs (Weeks 11–16)

| Week | Grammar | Extras |
|---|---|---|
| 11 | [Present tense](grammar/07-verbs-present.md) — both conjugations | Verb drills §1 |
| 12 | [Past & future](grammar/08-verbs-past-future.md) | Verb drills §2 |

**Self-check:** narrate your morning in past tense with correct aspect choices.

## Phase 4 — Fluency Building (Weeks 17–24)

| Week | Focus |
|---|---|
| 17 | [Numbers & time](grammar/12-numbers-time.md) |
| 18–19 | [Verbs of motion — intro](grammar/13-verbs-of-motion.md) (идти/ходить, ехать/ездить only) |
| 20–24 | Output phase: [translation exercises](practice/translation-exercises.md), graded readers, comprehensible input ([resources](resources.md)), first tutoring sessions if possible |

**Self-check (A2):** hold a 5-minute conversation about yourself, your day, and your plans; read a short news item for children and summarize it.

## Rules of thumb

- **Never skip drills.** Reading grammar ≠ knowing grammar.
- **Aspect and genitive get double time.** They're the two walls everyone hits.
- **Speak from week 1**, even alone — reading phrases aloud counts.
- Flashcards daily, everything else can flex around your schedule.
